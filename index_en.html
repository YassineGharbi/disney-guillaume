Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+ ?+

Disney+ is the destination for all Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic,Star and more.

What can I watch on Disney+ ? +

Disney+ offers a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and films
  • Timeless classics and recent successes from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Endless entertainment with comedies, dramas, stories inspired by real events and many other contents
  • Moving stories for all ages animated by talented artists from Pixar
  • The entire Star Wars saga and a large part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • National Geographic documentaries, special productions, and much more.

What is the price of the Disney+ subscription? +

You can choose among three subscription levels on Disney+:

  • Premium (11,99 €/month or 119,90 €/year): Resolution up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR. 4 simultaneous views. Downloads for offline viewing up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound on compatible content. No advertising.
  • Standard (8,99 €/month or 89,90 €/year): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous views. Downloads for offline playback on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content. Ad-free.
  • Standard with ads (5.99 €/month): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous playbacks. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content.

At the end of your subscription period, your subscription will automatically renew for the same period at the then-current Premium rate (currently those listed above), unless you cancel it beforehand or select a different offer.

Does the Disney+ subscription include a commitment period?+

Disney+ is without commitment and you can cancel your subscription whenever you want. Cancellation will be effective at the end of the period for which you have paid. To cancel your subscription, follow these 6 steps:

  1. Log in at www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel subscription
  6. Select Continue to cancel
